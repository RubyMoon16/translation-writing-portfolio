## 🌌 Overview
*Raiz Fria – The Collapse of Reality* is a **choice-based cyberpunk RPG** set in **Neonspire**, a fractured city where biotech, AI, and authoritarian control collide.  
Players take on the role of **Helene**, a hybrid protagonist bound to the mystical **Frost Root**, navigating alliances and betrayals that will reshape the city’s fate.  

## 🔑 Key Features
- Branching narrative with **multiple endings**  
- Faction-based politics:  
  - **RHYLUMIX** – AI revolution  
  - **ECOSOPHIA ROOT** – nature-tech symbiosis  
  - **SENTINEL** – military order  
- Moral dilemmas with lasting impact  
- Immersive cyberpunk worldbuilding
